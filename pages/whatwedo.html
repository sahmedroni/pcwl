Sourcing

Our expert team source fabrics and trims globally with a focus on efficiency and ethics. Our strong partnerships with suppliers are the key to a fast supply chain and ensure that we offer traceability of raw materials. We carry out site visits and welcome suppliers to our production facilities in return. We only work with mills that that are ZDHC compliant and undertake our own assessments. A large percentage of our suppliers are local or from near neighbour countries meaning we can offer both efficiency in transit and reduced carbon footprints per product.

Development

We deliver a seamless experience for brands with a range of production needs. We are industry leaders in merchandising to price points and for diverse fashion sensibilities. We offer a full service from design through to production working closely with our clients across the product development lifecycle to ensure that standards of quality and sampling lead times are met. Our advanced sampling facilities and extensive experience mean that we are able to develop a wide range of products making us the one-stop-shop for the world's biggest brands.

Manufacture

We are specialists in premium garment manufacture with an uncompromising focus on quality while seamless supply chain management and forwarding services make us cost effective. Our expertise combined with advanced machinery and technological capabilities means that we deliver the most technical products on the market.Our ability to manufacture multiple categories under one roof is unique. This combined with our strict ethical code is the reason we remain the first point of call for a loyal international client base of buyers.
